About Us

At some point, we’ve all experienced the stress of losing something important. What if there were a smarter, simpler way to get it back — one that brings people together in the process?

Welcome to our intelligent Lost & Found solution, designed for the MMU community. Using dynamic QR codes, we help you protect and recover your belongings — efficiently, securely, and with a touch of innovation.

How It Works

  • Generate a unique QR code and print it in any size you prefer
  • Track total scans, time, and date of each scan
  • Monitor your item’s scan history in real-time

Share with Trust

You can safely share tracking access with trusted individuals. This collaborative feature helps others assist you while keeping your privacy intact.

Campus-Centric Tracking

Tracking is limited to the MMU campus for accuracy and safety. Scans appear on a real-time campus map so you always know the latest location.

Reward Those Who Help

Users can create a commission when reporting a lost item. Once the item is found, the commission can be closed — turning support into success.

When an Item is Found

  • The finder can return it directly
  • Or share the item’s location for pickup

Enhancing Every Detail

  • Upload a photo for easy recognition
  • Add notes or descriptions to identify the item
  • Get instant notifications when your QR code is scanned
  • Browse the public “Found Items” list

Built for MMU — Powered by Community

More than a tool — this is a trusted network of students and staff working together. We make finding what’s lost faster, easier, and more meaningful.

Because sometimes, it takes a village — or just one good scan.
