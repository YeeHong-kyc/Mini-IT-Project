🔎 About Us

Ever lost something and wished there was a better way to find it? We’re here to make that easier.

📱 How It Works

  • Track the number of scans
  • See the time and date of each scan
  • Know when and where your item was last interacted with

🤝 Share Access, Work Together

Share tracking access securely with others to:

  • Increase chances of finding your item
  • Build teamwork and community bonding

🗺 On-Campus Tracking

Our live map feature shows scan locations, available only within MMU campus for privacy and accuracy.

💸 Commission System

Lost something? Set a reward to encourage help from others and end it once your item is found.

🔄 When Someone Finds an Item

  • Return it directly to you
  • Share the item’s location so you can pick it up

🧩 Extra Features

  • 📸 Upload item photos for easier recognition
  • 🔔 Get scan notifications in real time
  • 📝 Add item descriptions and notes
  • 📋 Public list of found items for quick checks

🎓 Built for the MMU Community

Our system is designed to help MMU students and staff protect their belongings — faster and smarter.
